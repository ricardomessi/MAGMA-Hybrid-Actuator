Core Technology

Two systems.
One actuator.

MAGMA combines contactless coaxial magnetic gearing with Nitinol shape memory alloy position locking — eliminating gear wear and reducing holding power by 93%.

Coaxial Magnetic Gear

4 inner / 17 outer pole pairs with 21 SMC modulation segments. Contactless torque multiplication at 95% efficiency. Built-in overload protection via magnetic slip.
