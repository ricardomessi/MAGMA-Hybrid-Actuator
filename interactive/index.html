HOW IT WORKS

Motion Mode: BLDC motor spins the high-speed rotor (4 pole pairs). Magnetic field couples through the 21-segment modulation ring to drive the low-speed rotor (17 pole pairs) at 4.25× torque multiplication — all contactless.

Hold Mode: 6 Nitinol SMA wires wrap the brake drum. 0.5A current heats them past 78°C → crystal structure shifts from Martensite to Austenite → wires contract 4% (6mm) → clamp the shaft with 100 Nm holding torque. Motor shuts off. Hold power: only 3.5W.
